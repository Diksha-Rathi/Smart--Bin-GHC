    About Us

    We are a Bangalore based NGO. We are working closely with the Municipal Corporation of Bangalore regarding collection and segregation of waste. We have over 2000 bins installed all over the city of Bangalore. Our Volunteers are not only responsible for cleaning and refilling the bins but also we teach the fellow citizens about waste segregation. This website is our tool to help our volunteers with the same.
